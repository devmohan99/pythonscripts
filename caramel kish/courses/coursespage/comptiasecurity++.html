Comptia Security++

CompTIA Security+ is a global certification that validates the baseline skills you need to perform core security functions and pursue an IT security career.

What You'll Learn

  • ✔ Passing the CompTIA Security+ certification exam with confidence
  • ✔ Understand computer security, its functions, and its components
  • ✔ Become an effective security technician in a business environment
  • ✔ Security+ focuses on the latest trends and techniques in risk management, risk mitigation, threat management and intrusion detection.
  • ✔ Security+ emphasizes hands-on practical skills, ensuring the security professional is better prepared to problem solve a wider variety of issues.
  • ✔ More choose Security+ for DoD 8570 compliance than any other certification.
